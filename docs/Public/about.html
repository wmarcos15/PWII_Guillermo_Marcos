Residence - From Alicante to Madrid
Born amidst the sun-kissed shores of Alicante and now embracing the vibrant beat of Madrid, my journey spans the rich tapestry of Spain. Alicante, with its azure Mediterranean waters and historic charm, laid the foundation of my roots. Now, in the heart of Madrid, I find myself immersed in the dynamic rhythm of city life. From the enchanting old town of Alicante to the bustling energy of Madrid's streets, my story unfolds against the backdrop of diverse landscapes and cultures. Two cities, each weaving a unique thread into the fabric of my identity, creating a narrative that bridges the warmth of the coast with the vivacity of the capital.
